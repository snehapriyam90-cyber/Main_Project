Cancellation & Refund Policy

Welcome to Kisan Fresh. Your satisfaction is our priority, and we take great care in sourcing, packing, and delivering fresh fruits and vegetables. We understand that certain situations may require order cancellation or resolution, and this policy outlines the process clearly.

Order Cancellation Policy

Customers may request order cancellation before the order is shipped or within three hours of placing the order, whichever occurs first. Once an order is confirmed, partial cancellations or removal of specific items are not permitted. To request cancellation, please contact us via phone or email.

For prepaid orders cancelled within the allowed time frame, a full refund will be initiated after verification. Approved refunds are generally processed within 24 to 48 business hours. Kisan Fresh reserves the right to refuse cancellation or delay refunds in cases of repeated bulk cancellations or misuse of services.

Refund Policy

Due to the perishable nature of fruits and vegetables, refunds are not applicable once the order has been delivered. However, in exceptional cases where a refund is approved, the amount will be processed within five working days through the original payment method.

Damaged or Spoiled Products

If you receive products that are damaged, spoiled, or unfit for consumption, please notify us within 24 hours of delivery along with clear photo or video proof of unboxing. After evaluation, we may arrange a replacement or provide store credit based on the situation.

Incorrect or Missing Items

In case of incorrect or missing items in your order, please inform us within 24 hours of delivery. Our support team will verify the issue and arrange appropriate resolution such as replacement or store credit. Providing unboxing images or videos will help us resolve the issue faster.

No Returns Policy

Kisan Fresh does not accept returns for opened, used, or partially consumed products due to hygiene and food safety concerns. Once delivered, products cannot be returned under normal circumstances.

Shipping Charges

For orders below ₹300, a shipping fee of ₹50 is applicable. Cash on Delivery (COD) orders incur an additional charge of ₹50. Shipping charges are non-refundable once the order is dispatched.

Delivery Timeline

After order processing, our delivery partners such as DTDC, India Post, or Delhivery will collect your order from our warehouse. Delivery usually takes between 3 to 7 working days depending on your location.

Additional Information

Kisan Fresh is not responsible for any damage caused after delivery. Compensation in monetary form is generally not provided. Any approved refunds or credits are processed strictly as per our policy.
